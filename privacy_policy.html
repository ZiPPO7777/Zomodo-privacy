Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Privacy First: Zomodoro is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Zomodoro ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you use our Pomodoro timer application. Zomodoro is a productivity application that helps you manage your time using the Pomodoro Technique.

The short version: We don't collect any personal data. Period. Your productivity data, tasks, settings, and statistics remain entirely on your device and are never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Zomodoro is designed to be completely private. We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Personal Information: Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any identifying information
  • Usage Data: How you use the app, session durations, or productivity patterns
  • Task Data: Your tasks, task names, or any content you enter
  • Analytics: No tracking pixels, analytics services, or usage statistics
  • Device Information: Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data
  • Cookies: No tracking cookies or similar technologies

3. Local Data Storage

Zomodoro stores data locally on your device using browser localStorage to provide functionality. This includes:

Data Type Purpose Storage Location
Timer Settings Remember your preferred focus/break durations Your device only
Tasks Store your task list and progress Your device only
Statistics Track your Pomodoro completion counts Your device only
Theme Preferences Remember your visual theme choice Your device only
Achievement Progress Track unlocked achievements Your device only
Important: This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted anywhere. If you clear your browser data or uninstall the app, this information will be permanently deleted.

Zomodoro may request the following permissions to provide functionality:

  • Notifications: To alert you when Pomodoro sessions end. This is optional and can be disabled.
  • Local Storage: To save your settings, tasks, and progress on your device.
  • Audio: To play notification sounds and focus sounds. This is optional.

All permissions are used solely for app functionality and no data is transmitted externally.

6. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services. Zomodoro operates completely independently without:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)

7. Data Security

Since no data leaves your device, your information is as secure as your device itself. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device software updated
  • Using device security features (screen lock, encryption)
  • Being cautious about who has access to your device

8. Children's Privacy

Zomodoro is safe for users of all ages. We do not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Parents can feel confident that no personal data is collected or transmitted when their children use our app.

9. Data Portability & Deletion

Your Data, Your Control:

  • Delete: Clear your browser data or app storage to permanently delete all Zomodoro data
  • Transfer: Since data is stored locally, it doesn't automatically transfer between devices
